About ART-DE-LIGHT

ART-DE-LIGHT — the Art of Light, Glass, and Emotion

Since childhood, I have been fascinated by stained-glass windows in historic buildings. I could spend hours watching how sunlight passed through the colored glass, scattering vivid reflections across the walls and filling the space with a warm, almost magical glow. These windows came alive through light alone — long before electricity existed.

That feeling — the wonder of simple sunlight transforming space — stayed with me throughout my life.

Many years later, in a world where artificial light is always available, an idea emerged: What if I could bring that ancient beauty into a contemporary form? What if I could create art that breathes with light — both day and night?

That is how the story of ART-DE-LIGHT began.

A Modern Interpretation of Inspiration

I started working with glass, epoxy resin, contour lines, and internal lighting — blending craftsmanship, design, and technology. Each piece carries the transparency of stained glass, the layered depth of resin, and the gentle illumination of a built-in light-box that reveals the artwork from within.

Light became my “artificial sun,” allowing the piece to be admired at any moment.

A Dual Nature: With Light and Without It

My artworks have two distinct expressions:

  • Without the light-box — they appear as expressive standalone paintings.
  • With the light-box turned on — they transform into radiant light panels that reshape the atmosphere of the room.

This dynamic nature makes each piece feel alive. They can enhance any interior — a home, office, lounge, hotel, or gallery — and instantly change the mood of a space.

Handcrafted and Truly Unique

Every ART-DE-LIGHT piece is created entirely by hand, using techniques that make each artwork one of a kind. The flow of epoxy resin, the texture of the glass, the interplay of colors, and the hand-shaped contours ensure that no piece can ever be replicated exactly.

Even if I attempt to recreate a design, the natural behavior of the materials and the creative process itself make each artwork unique, with its own personality and energy.

This uniqueness is at the very heart of the brand.

Both Artwork and Light Object

ART-DE-LIGHT pieces are designed to function on two levels:

  • as a painting, viewed as a traditional artwork,
  • and as an illuminated panel, serving a decorative and functional purpose.

This duality makes the artwork a part of the architecture — not just decoration.

My Style: Contours, Color, and Depth

A defining element of my technique is the use of thin wire to create clean, sculptural contours. These lines:

  • enhance clarity and expression,
  • structure the composition,
  • separate and protect the colors of the resin,
  • and create a modern stained-glass-inspired effect.

They act as a guiding framework of light — shaping the artwork and giving it depth.

The Philosophy of ART-DE-LIGHT

I believe that art should not only decorate — it should energize. Each of my pieces reflects an emotion, a memory, a journey, or a moment of inner clarity. My intention is to create art that brings warmth, harmony, and inspiration into any space.

My guiding principles:

  • ✨ Art should bring joy.
  • ✨ Art should offer tranquility or spark emotion.
  • ✨ Art should illuminate — both visually and spiritually.
  • ✨ Art should become part of one’s living space and inner world.
  • ✨ And above all: art should be authentic and unique.

The name ART-DE-LIGHT was born from this belief: art that brings light.

If one of my works resonates with someone’s heart, the mission is fulfilled.
